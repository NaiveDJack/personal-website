Personal statement

A starsailor, finding the right question

I travelled a bit physically. I have travelled a lot more through the stories I've heard. I would like to live like this, all my life. To visit all the people I love around the world. Hear new stories and live new ones. And above all, to build digital tools and spaces, to give back what I have been fortunate enough to have.

The unique mix of my background allows me to speak the languages of different professions, and connect them at a time when many specialisations are discovering they can learn from each other.

Knowledge is literally a currency, in organisations large and small. I have always developed systems to circulate it as much as possible, while respecting confidentiality and security.

Listening to stories means understanding others beyond mere words, and this quality is useful in individual human relationships, in teams, and in research. It is all about, after all, knowing how to find the right question.
